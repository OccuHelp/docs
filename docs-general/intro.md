# Introduction

A central location for all **OccuHelp documentation**.

## What's Included

- **Quick Access**: Dashboard, new entries, activity reports, employee files, and insights
- **Risk Analysis**: Job analysis, task analysis, site analysis, program analysis, and risk management
- **Prevention**: Ergonomics, employment testing, physical demands analysis, prehab, stretching & strengthening, and wellness
- **Medical**: Injury/illness tracking, case management, early intervention, MSK rehab, and self-care
- **Operations**: Incident manager, inventory, OSHA recordkeeping, quality assurance, and safety data sheets
- **Extras**: Calendar, chat, and notifications
- **Settings**: Profile management, company settings, and admin center
